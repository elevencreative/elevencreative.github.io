Photography, Software Development, Web Development, Graphic Design

We are a husband and wife creative team, based in Mid-Michigan, specializing in photography. In the blink of an eye, the moments are gone- we can capture those moments, for you to keep for a lifetime. Although we have drastically different backgrounds (graphic design vs. computer science), we both can at least agree on a passion for photography. We're both constantly seeking new challenges, which is what keeps things fun. We love photographing portraits, places, products, families, sports, and pretty much anything else we can point our cameras at.
